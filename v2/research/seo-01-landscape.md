# SEO Landscape: Top 20 English-Language China Travel Websites for Foreign Visitors

**Research date:** May 2026
**Methodology:** SimilarWeb April 2026 data, Ahrefs public previews (ahrefstop.com), SEMrush free overview, `site:` operator Google counts, and direct page fetches. All traffic figures are third-party estimates — tools use different methodologies and diverge on the same domain. Where a range appeared, the midpoint is used. Monthly traffic = most recent single-month figure unless stated.

---

## Site 1 — Trip.com (China travel section)

**URL:** https://www.trip.com/china-travel/ (guide hub at /guide/)
**Organisation type:** OTA — Ctrip Group, NASDAQ-listed
**Founded:** 1999 (Ctrip); Trip.com international brand launched ~2017
**Foreign-traveller focus:** Secondary — full-service global OTA; China content is one section within a global platform

### Traffic (whole domain)
| Metric | Value | Source |
|--------|-------|--------|
| Monthly visits | 140.7 M | SimilarWeb Apr 2026 |
| Organic share of keyword traffic | 66 % | SimilarWeb |
| Ahrefs DR | 86 | AhrefsTop |
| Monthly organic visits | ~9 M | AhrefsTop |
| Ranked keywords | ~3.8 M | AhrefsTop |
| Traffic value | $1.7 M/mo | AhrefsTop |

### Top ranking keywords (domain-wide, China examples)
- "trip" — 28.6 K visits/mo, rank #1
- "how to use alipay in china for foreigners" — top 3 (trip.com/guide/phone/how-to-use-alipay.html)
- "china visa free transit" — ranking (trip.com/guide/visa/china-visa-free-transit.html)
- "china esim" — top 5 (trip.com/guide/phone/china-esim.html)
- "china travel tips" — top 5

### Indexed pages
`site:trip.com/guide china` returns hundreds of China guide articles: arrival card, entry requirements, cost calculator, eSIM, payment, transport, itineraries.

### Content focus
Flights, hotels, trains, tours, attraction tickets (booking-first), plus a growing editorial /guide section covering visa, payment, transport, food, and itineraries for China.

### Unique angle / positioning
World's largest OTA for Asia with end-to-end booking. The /guide content competes organically with pure-editorial sites while converting directly to bookings. Aggressive recent content investment in practical foreigner-to-China topics: Alipay setup, visa-free transit, arrival card rules, eSIM with VPN.

## Site 2 — Lonely Planet (China)

**URL:** https://www.lonelyplanet.com/china
**Organisation type:** Publisher / Travel Guide (Red Ventures-owned since 2022)
**Founded:** 1973 (print); lonelyplanet.com online mid-1990s
**Foreign-traveller focus:** Core — general travel guide, China is one destination chapter

### Traffic
| Metric | Value | Source |
|--------|-------|--------|
| Monthly visits | 2.1–2.2 M | SimilarWeb / SEMrush Apr 2026 |
| Organic share | 64–97 % | Both tools |
| Ahrefs DR | 89 | AhrefsTop |
| SEMrush Authority Score | 77 | SEMrush |
| Monthly organic visits | ~1.6 M | AhrefsTop |
| Referring domains | 73.6 K | AhrefsTop |

### Top ranking keywords
- "lonely planet" — 13.4 K/mo, rank #1
- "cinque terre" — 26.7 K, rank #1 (shows destination-level authority)
- China section: "best places to visit in china", "great wall china", "beijing travel guide", "xi'an travel", "shanghai travel guide"

### Indexed pages
 returns the destination hub plus city sub-pages (Beijing, Shanghai, Xi'an, Chengdu, Guilin, Hangzhou, Guangzhou, Shenzhen, Chongqing), attraction listings, articles — estimated 500+ China-specific indexed pages.

### Content focus
Destination overviews, top attractions, itineraries, when to go, transport, accommodation. Limited depth on China-specific practicalities (Alipay setup, VPN, transit visa procedures).

### Unique angle / positioning
Brand trust among Western travellers provides strong legacy-link authority. Since the 2022 Red Ventures acquisition and withdrawal from the Chinese-language market, editorial investment in China content has slowed markedly. The site now ranks primarily on domain authority and legacy links rather than fresh content — a gap a new entrant can exploit with current material.

### Note
Lonely Planet permanently closed its Thorn Tree travel forum in September 2021. A successor community (thorntreeforum.com) attracts only ~4.9 K visits/month and is negligible as an SEO competitor.
